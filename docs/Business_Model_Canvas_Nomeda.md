# Business Model Canvas: Nomeda

## 1. Key Partnerships
* **AI and API Providers:** For LLM, STT, TTS.
* **Cloud and GPU Providers.**
* **Mental Health Specialists and Academic Partners.**
* **Open Source AI Communities and Researchers.**

## 2. Key Activities
* **Developing and Training AI Models:** Processing voice input with Speech-to-Text (STT).
* **Detecting Emotions:** Analyzing voice and face expression patterns.
* **Integrating Multimodal AI Systems:** Merging the RAG-based therapy knowledge system.
* **Generating Real-Time Personalized Responses:** Improving conversation and user experience.
* **Ensuring Privacy and Security.**

## 3. Key Resources
* **AI and Intelligent Systems.**
* **Therapy Knowledge Resources.**
* **Emotion Analyzer Tools.**
* **Cloud and Computing Infrastructure.**
* **Secure Data Management.**
* **Audio and Visual Devices.**
* **Development Tools and Platforms.**

## 4. Value Propositions
* **24/7 AI-Powered Therapeutic Support.**
* **Real-time Emotion and Behavior Analysis.**
* **Personalized Therapy Conversations.**
* **Natural Voice-Based Interaction Experience.**
* **Integrated STT, Emotion Detection, and AI Reasoning.**
* **Private and Accessible Mental Support.**
* **Continuous Supportive Communication.**
* **Fast, Scalable, and Cost-Effective Therapy Alternative.**

## 5. Customer Relationships
* **AI-Powered Therapy:** Through voice and camera interaction.
* **Tone Analysis:** Analysis of speech, facial emotion, and voice tone.
* **Personalized Emotional Support:** Based on user state.
* **Private One-to-One AI Therapist Experience.**
* **Continuous Empathetic Companion and Guidance.**
* **24/7 Accessible Mental Wellness Support.**

## 6. Channels
* **Website.**
* **Apple App Store.**
* **Google Play Store.**

## 7. Customer Segments
* **People experiencing stress or psychological pressure.**
* **University Students.**
* **People who need quick emotional support.**
* **People who cannot afford expensive therapy.**
* **Companies that care about employee mental health.**
* **Teenagers and Young Adults.**

* **Cloud Hosting and GPU Expenses.**

## 9. Revenue Streams
* **Monthly and Yearly Subscription Plans.**
* **Freemium Model:** With paid premium features.
* **Enterprise Licensing:** For schools and clinics.
